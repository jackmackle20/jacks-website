In early 2021 I made my first investment in the uranium sector following some advice from a friend.
Overtime, I developed a lot of interest for the commodity and the nuclear space in general.
I found Twitter (X), to be the best place to stay in the know.

I wanted to contribute to the uranium community and work on some data science skills.
So I created a bot that scraped posts about uranium and output a daily average sentiment value.
I used VADER, a sentiment analysis tool and ran the bot through a VM on Digital Ocean.

Scraping X used to be simple through the API, but Elon has since cracked down on that and bots in general.
